FEE STRUCTURE

--- MAIN CAMPUS FEE COMPONENTS ---

FEE CATEGORIES EXPLANATION:
- One Time Charges: Paid only once at the time of admission.
  - Admission Fee: One-time fee for admission processing.
  - Caution Money: Refundable security deposit.
- Per Semester Charges: Recurring fees paid every semester.
  - IT Charges: Fee for information technology services.
  - Miscellaneous Charges: General university service fees.
  - Examination Charges (Per Course): Fee for each course's examination.
  - Lab Charges (Per Credit Hour): Fee for lab-based courses, charged per lab credit hour.
  - Tuition Fee (Per Credit Hour): Academic instruction fee per lecture credit hour.
