FEE STRUCTURE AND REGULATIONS: Dawood University of Engineering & Technology (DUET)

--- ADMISSION PROCESSING FEE (PAYABLE ONCE) ---

For Local Students (PKR):
- Admission Fee: PKR 5,000
- Security Deposit (Refundable): PKR 5,000
- **Total Admission Processing Fee: PKR 10,000**

For Foreign Students (USD):
- Admission Fee: USD 250 (Equivalent PKR)
- **Total Admission Processing Fee: USD 250**

(Applies to all B.S / B.E / B.Arch / M.S / Ph.D. students)

--- SEMESTER FEE FOR LOCAL STUDENTS (PKR) ---

*PhD Students:*
- Semester Registration Fee: PKR 6,000
- Tuition Fee per Credit Hour: PKR 5,000
- Transport/Fuel Charges (Optional): Not Applicable

*MS Students:*
- Semester Registration Fee: PKR 6,000
- Tuition Fee per Credit Hour: PKR 5,000
- Transport/Fuel Charges (Optional): Not Applicable

*All Undergraduate (UG) Students (B.S/B.E/B.Arch):*
- Semester Registration Fee: PKR 6,000
- Tuition Fee per Credit Hour: PKR 3,700
- Transport/Fuel Charges (Optional): PKR 7,000

--- SEMESTER FEE FOR FOREIGN STUDENTS (USD) ---

*PhD Students:*
- Semester Fee: USD 200
- Tuition Fee per Credit Hour: USD 100
- Transport/Fuel Charges (Optional): USD 50

*MS Students:*
- Semester Fee: USD 200
- Tuition Fee per Credit Hour: USD 100
- Transport/Fuel Charges (Optional): USD 50

*All Undergraduate (UG) Students:*
- Semester Fee: USD 150
- Tuition Fee per Credit Hour: USD 70
- Transport/Fuel Charges (Optional): USD 50

**IMPORTANT NOTES ON SEMESTER FEE:**
1. Students pay based on the number of courses/credit hours registered.
2. Foreign students pay in PKR equivalent to the USD fee.
3. Challan validity is 45 days from semester start. Late payment incurs a 10% penalty.
4. The Dean may recommend a waiver or installment plan for financial hardship, subject to VC approval.
5. Transcripts/degrees will be withheld for unpaid fees.
6. Transport charges apply only to students using university transport services.

--- SELF-FINANCE FEE (ONE-TIME PAYMENT AT ADMISSION) ---

*For Local Students (PKR + Taxes):*
- B.E. in Electronics Engineering or Computer System Engineering: PKR 600,000
- B.E. in Chemical Engineering, Industrial Engineering & Management, or B.Arch.: PKR 400,000
- B.E. in Telecommunication, Petroleum & Gas, Metallurgy & Materials, or Energy & Environment: PKR 300,000
- B.S. in Computer Science, Cyber Security, or Artificial Intelligence: PKR 400,000

*For Foreign Students (USD + Taxes):*
- B.E. in Electronics Engineering or Computer System Engineering: USD 4,000
- B.E. in Chemical Engineering, Industrial Engineering & Management, or B.Arch.: USD 3,000
- B.E. in Telecommunication, Petroleum & Gas, Metallurgy & Materials, or Energy & Environment: USD 2,500
- B.S. in Computer Science, Cyber Security, or Artificial Intelligence: USD 3,000

**RULES FOR SELF-FINANCE FEE:**
1. Applicants applying to multiple disciplines under self-finance must deposit the fee for only one discipline. The highest applicable amount must be deposited.
2. The Admission Committee Chair may allow payment in up to four equal installments upon written request. Each installment's challan is valid for 45 days from its due date, with a 5% late penalty.
3. The fee will be refunded if the candidate is not admitted.
4. Self-finance/foreign students must still pay regular University semester fees for their program.

--- MS/PhD PROGRAM SPECIFIC CHARGES ---
1. PhD students pay the Semester Registration Fee during their entire research period.
2. The Semester Registration Fee applies for the full study period of MS/PhD.
3. During research work, students pay PKR 10,000 per semester for research supervision and lab access.
4. All fees except the Security Deposit are non-refundable.

--- FEE REFUND POLICY ---
1. The University follows HEC/DUET Fee Refund Policy.
2. 100% semester fee is refunded if a provisional admission student (waiting for results) does not meet criteria, provided they apply within 3 weeks of result announcement.
3. The Admission Fee is non-refundable under any circumstances.

--- WITHHOLDING TAX COLLECTION ---
1. As per FBR Act 2020, a 5% withholding tax applies if the total fee exceeds PKR 200,000 and the payer is not on FBR's Active Taxpayers List.
2. Overseas Pakistanis/Expatriates may be exempt if they provide:
   - Passport copy proving stay in Pakistan was less than 182 days in the tax year.
   - Certificate stating they have no Pakistani source of income.
   - Proof that fee was remitted directly from abroad to the university's bank account.
3. The Security Deposit is refunded after degree completion upon providing a clearance certificate.

--- MIGRATION FEE ---

*For Local Students (Migration into DUET):*
- Fee: PKR 400,000 + Taxes (Non-refundable)
- Payment Method: Pay Order in favor of "Director Finance, Dawood University of Engineering and Technology, Karachi"
- Deadline: Within 2 weeks of receiving notice from the Registrar's Office.

*For Foreign Students (Migration into DUET):*
- Fee: PKR 600,000 + Taxes (Non-refundable)
- Payment Method: Pay Order in favor of "Director Finance, Dawood University of Engineering and Technology, Karachi"
- Deadline: Within 2 weeks of receiving notice from the Registrar's Office.

*For Students Migrating OUT from DUET:*
- Processing Fee: PKR 5,000 (Rupees five thousand only)

--- GENERAL UNIVERSITY POLICY ---
The University reserves the right to change the fee structure at any time.
