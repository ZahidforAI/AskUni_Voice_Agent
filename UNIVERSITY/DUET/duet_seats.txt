--- IMPORTANT POLICY NOTES ---

1. Fourteen (14) total seats are reserved for DUET teaching and non-teaching employees (7+7). Vacant seats in these categories are interchangeable.
2. All vacant seats after exhausting admission formalities shall be distributed on "open merit" after the 3rd merit list.
3. The admission committee may consider requests for admission in other technologies, subject to seat availability.
4. The competent authority reserves the right to change seat allocation or policy at any time without notice.
5. For BS Programs: The admission committee has discretion to fill vacant seats across categories based on merit or leave them unfilled, subject to department capacity and VC approval.
