Duet Currently offers Kamran Education Foundation Scholarship

This is to inform that the Financial Assistance Department (FAD) of DUET is inviting needy and deserving students to apply for a scholarship from the Kamran Educational Foundation (KEF). Students with a sound academic track record who are enrolled on merit seats are eligible to apply for this scholarship.

1. Eligibility:

Students admitted on merit seats.

A minimum GPA of 3.0 in the last semester, and for those students whose results were not declared, at least 65% in Intermediate.

All students in the B.S. and B.Arch degree programs (Batch 23f, 22f, 21f, 20f, and 19f for B.Arch only) who fulfill the aforementioned criteria are eligible to apply for this scholarship.

2. Ineligibility:

Self-finance students.

Students getting or committed to any financial aid/scholarship from any other organization will not be eligible to apply.

Students who have failed in any subject in a semester are not eligible to apply.
