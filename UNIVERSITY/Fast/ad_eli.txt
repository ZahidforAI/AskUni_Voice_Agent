Here is the admission information for FAST-NUCES undergraduate programs:

Undergraduate Programs

Those who have taken the HSSC or an equivalent examination and are awaiting result are also eligible to apply. The four-year undergraduate programs of full time study are divided into eight semesters.

1. BS (Engineering Programs)

Engineering Degree Programs:
- Bachelor of Science (Civil Engineering)
- Bachelor of Science (Electrical Engineering)
- Bachelor of Science (Computer Engineering)

Admission Test Options (Select One):
- FAST-NUCES
- SAT
- NTS NAT-IE (Note: NTS test from specific universities will not be acceptable)

Eligibility Requirements:
1. SSC (Matric) or equivalent: 60% minimum marks
2. HSSC (FSc) or equivalent: 60% minimum marks
3. Courses studied at HSSC or equivalent level: Pre-Engg (Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics) OR ICS (Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics)

Selection Criteria Weightage:
- Admission Test marks: 33%
- HSSC/equivalent marks: 50%
- SSC/equivalent marks: 17%

Weightage of HSSC marks shall be calculated based on (whichever is applicable) at the time of merit list compilation:
- Marks obtained in HSSC part I and II, OR
- Marks obtained in HSSC part I if HSSC part II not available, OR
- Marks/Percentage given in IBCC equivalence certificate of A-level, OR
- Marks/Percentage given in IBCC equivalence certificate of O-level
- Merit cut-off marks to be determined by the University

Note: All documents/transcripts will be checked at admission. Any incorrect/false information or attempt to hide information will lead to disqualification.

2. BS (Computing Programs)

Computing Degree Programs:
- Bachelor of Science (Artificial Intelligence)
- Bachelor of Science (Computer Science)
- Bachelor of Science (Cyber Security)
- Bachelor of Science (Data Science)
- Bachelor of Science (Software Engineering)

Admission Test Options (Select One):
- FAST-NUCES
- SAT
- NTS NAT-IE, NAT-ICS (Note: NTS test from specific universities will not be acceptable)

Eligibility Requirements:
1. SSC (Matric) or equivalent: 60% minimum marks
2. HSSC (FSc) or equivalent: 50% minimum marks
3. Courses studied at HSSC or equivalent level: Mathematics

Selection Criteria Weightage:
- Admission Test marks: 50%
- HSSC/equivalent marks: 40%
- SSC/equivalent marks: 10%

Computing Domain Note: Pre-medical students who have passed additional maths or are waiting for results are eligible to apply for admission in computing programs.

Weightage of HSSC marks shall be calculated based on (whichever is applicable) at the time of merit list compilation:
- Marks obtained in HSSC part I and II, OR
- Marks obtained in HSSC part I if result of HSSC part II not available, OR
- Marks/Percentage given in IBCC equivalence certificate of A-level, OR
- Marks/Percentage given in IBCC equivalence certificate of O-level
- Merit cut-off marks to be determined by the University

Note: All documents/transcripts will be checked at admission. Any incorrect/false information or attempt to hide information will lead to disqualification.
