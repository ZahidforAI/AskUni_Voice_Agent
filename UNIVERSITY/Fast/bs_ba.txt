Program Vision:
To be a leading undergraduate program in business analytics, empowering students with cutting-edge analytical skills, innovative thinking, and a strong ethical foundation to thrive in a dynamic, technology-driven global economy.

Program Mission:
The FSM BS (BA) program aims to develop aspiring professionals with the expertise to drive data-informed decision-making by integrating business management, technology, and analytics. The program emphasizes ethical responsibility, sustainability, and the development of communication, analytical, and problem-solving skills to deliver lasting value to the global economy and the society.

Career Opportunities:
Graduates of Bachelor of Science (Business Analytics) can select from several careers in analytics in the areas of business, operations, people, finance and marketing, where they can leverage their data analytics skills to translate structured and unstructured data into valuable business insights by identifying patterns, trends, and anomalies that inform strategic decisions, optimize operations, enhance customer experiences, and drive sustainable growth across the organization. The program equips graduates to work in cross-functional teams and provides a strong foundation for careers or further studies in business and analytics.

Award of Degree:
For the award of Bachelor of Science (Business Analytics) degree, a student must have:

Passed courses totalling at least 135 credit hours, including all those courses which have been specified as Core courses.
Obtained a CGPA of at least 2.00
