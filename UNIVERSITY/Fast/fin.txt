Program Vision:
To be a leading program that nurtures globally competent professionals capable of driving innovation in the financial technology sector by integrating financial expertise with cutting-edge technological solutions.

Program Mission:
The FSM FinTech program seeks to provide a comprehensive blend of finance, technology, and entrepreneurial knowledge, addressing the challenges posed by disruptive technologies in the financial services sector. With a focus on the ethical, regulatory, and societal implications of FinTech, the program aims to empower students to design and implement technology-driven solutions that tackle complex financial challenges, improve business efficiency, and contribute to sustainable economic growth.

Career Opportunities:
Graduates of the BS Financial Technology program can pursue roles such as blockchain specialist, financial data analyst, digital payments expert, robo-advisory associate, or fintech consultant. Other career paths include positions like cryptocurrency manager, RegTech analyst, and AI-driven financial strategist. Opportunities from this degree span sectors such as digital lending, open banking, and financial cybersecurity. The program prepares fintech professionals who can enhance operations, implement automation, and innovate using data analytics, artificial intelligence, and blockchain technologies in the ever-evolving financial sector.

Award of Degree:
For the award of Bachelor of Science (Financial Technology) degree, a student must have:

Passed courses totalling at least 138 credit hours, including all those courses which have been specified as Core courses.
Obtained a CGPA of at least 2.00
