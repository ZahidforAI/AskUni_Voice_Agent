OVERVIEW

The Institute of Business Administration (IBA) Karachi is one of Pakistan’s leading higher education institutions.
It offers undergraduate, graduate, postgraduate, and diploma programs in business, economics, social sciences, mathematics, and computer science.
Admissions at IBA are merit-based, and the institute emphasizes academic excellence, ethical leadership, research, and innovation.

ACADEMIC STRUCTURE

IBA Karachi operates through three main academic schools:

School of Business Studies (SBS)
School of Economics and Social Sciences (SESS)
School of Mathematics and Computer Science (SMCS)

Each school offers specialized degree programs supported by research centers, laboratories, and industry collaborations.

KEY FACTS AND STATISTICS

41 percent of students received financial assistance during the academic year 2023–2024.
IBA has more than 20,000 alumni serving in leading national and multinational organizations.
Approximately 70 percent of IBA faculty members hold PhD degrees.

RECENT ACADEMIC AND STUDENT ACHIEVEMENTS

Faculty members at IBA regularly publish research in international journals across business, economics, computer science, and social sciences.
Students and alumni have successfully defended PhD and MS theses, participated in global academic and policy forums, and earned international placements and scholarships.
IBA students have represented Pakistan in international academic, research, and climate policy platforms.

HISTORICAL OVERVIEW

The Institute of Business Administration Karachi was established in 1955 as the Institute of Public and Business Administration (IPBA).
It was founded as a USAID-financed business school modeled on the United States MBA system, making it one of the oldest business schools outside North America.

Initially affiliated with the University of Karachi, IBA gained degree-awarding status in 1994 through legislation passed by the Sindh Assembly.
This marked a major transformation in the institute’s academic autonomy and growth.

EVOLUTION OF ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

IBA began with MBA programs and gradually expanded its academic offerings to include undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral degrees.
Over time, the institute introduced BBA, BS Computer Science, BS Economics, MS, MBA, and PhD programs across business, economics, mathematics, computer science, journalism, and data science.

The establishment of specialized schools and research centers strengthened IBA’s role in higher education and research in Pakistan.

INFRASTRUCTURE AND INSTITUTIONAL DEVELOPMENT

IBA has developed modern campuses with academic buildings, research labs, libraries, student hostels, sports facilities, and innovation centers.
The institute has invested in sustainable infrastructure, including solar power plants and environmentally responsible campus development.
