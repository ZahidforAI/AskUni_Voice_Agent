ADMISSION POLICY OVERVIEW

IBA Karachi maintains high standards in student intake, classroom instruction, discipline, and assessment.
Admissions emphasize merit, quality, and alignment with international best practices.
The policy applies to all programs and ensures fair and transparent selection without quotas or reserved seats.

SALIENT POINTS

Admission is strictly merit-based, regardless of race, religion, gender, ethnicity, or socio-economic background.

No quotas or efforts to fill seats if candidates do not meet criteria.

Program-specific Aptitude Test cut-offs are determined by statistical analysis by the Admissions Committee.

A single Aptitude Test is conducted for both Main and City campuses.

Candidates may select specializations after:

2 years for undergraduate programs

1 year for graduate programs

Fee structure is identical for both campuses.

CONDITIONS FOR ADMISSION

Apply online and register for the Aptitude Test.

Pay required processing fee and obtain admit card.

Qualify in the Aptitude Test or provide SAT/ACT/GMAT/GRE scores for exemption.

Participate in Group Discussion (if applicable) and interview, clearing both.

Meet minimum academic eligibility and submit mark sheets/transcripts.

Provide equivalence certificates for degrees from non-Pakistani boards/universities.

APTITUDE TEST, GROUP DISCUSSION & INTERVIEWS

Aptitude Test: Evaluates quantitative & analytical skills, logical reasoning, communication, reading comprehension, lexical and grammatical ability, and program aptitude.

Group Discussion: Assesses academic background, leadership potential, extracurricular interests, communication, integrity, and enthusiasm.

Interviews: Examine qualities for academic success and potential societal contributions.

SAT EXEMPTION POLICY

Exemption is merit-based; direct admission/interview is not guaranteed solely by SAT scores.

SAT scores for exemption differ by program:

BBA & BSBA: ACT 29 (Composite), 25 (English/Writing)

BSSS: ACT 28 (Composite), 25 (English/Writing)

BSAF, BSECO, BSCS, BSEM, BSEDS: ACT 28 (Composite), English/Writing optional

Candidates encouraged to take IBA Aptitude Test for better admission chances.

PROGRAM-WISE ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

BBA, BS ACF & BSBA:

HSC: Minimum 65%

A' Levels: Minimum 2 'B's & 1 'C' in 3 principal subjects

American/Canadian High School: Minimum 80%

IB: Minimum 25 points out of 45

Equivalence certificate from IBCC for foreign credentials

BS Programs (CS, Economics & Mathematics, Mathematics & Economics & Data Science):

HSC Pre-Engineering or General with Math: Minimum 60%

A' Levels: Minimum 1 'B' & 2 'C's in 3 principal subjects

American/Canadian High School: Minimum 80%

IB: Minimum 24 points out of 45

Equivalence certificate from IBCC for foreign credentials

BS Programs (Economics & Social Sciences & Liberal Arts):

HSC: Minimum 60%

A' Levels: Minimum 1 'B' & 2 'C's

American/Canadian High School: Minimum 80%

IB: Minimum 24 points out of 45

Equivalence certificate from IBCC for foreign credentials

GRADUATE PROGRAMS

MS Programs:

Minimum 16 years of education (master’s degree or equivalent)

4 years in HEC recognized institute with ≥2.5 CGPA or 60%

Work experience preferred

GMAT: Minimum 600 / GRE: Quantitative 160 & Verbal 150 for test exemption

MBA / EMBA Programs:

Details at MBA
 and EMBA
 websites

PhD Programs:

MS/M.Phil/Equivalent from HEC recognized local/foreign university

Minimum 60% or 3.0 CGPA on 4.0 scale

Specific departmental requirements apply

Equivalence certificate required for other degrees

POSTGRADUATE DIPLOMA PROGRAMS

Supply Chain, HR, Project Management:

14 years education + 6 years post-qualification experience

16 years education + 3 years post-qualification experience

Healthcare Management:

Doctors (MBBS/BDS) with 2 years hospital experience

Nurses with 5 years hospital experience

Other healthcare professionals: 16 years education + 2 years hospital experience

COURSE CREDIT TRANSFER POLICY

Eligible for transfer from top 100 global universities (Shanghai Jiao Tong, THE, QS) or partner institutions like LUMS

Approval required for other universities

Minimum 50% of total degree credits must be completed at IBA

Only courses with grade 'B' or above are transferable

Core/minor courses require review and approval by relevant experts and Program Director

Transferred grades do not affect CGPA or positions/medals

ADMISSION PROCEDURE
ONLINE APPLICATION

Applications accepted via IBA Online Admissions System

Fill online Admission Application Form

Print bank challan and deposit processing fee at designated bank

Admit card sent via registered email and SMS

ISSUANCE OF ADMIT CARDS

Generated after fee payment

Download/print via login using ID & password

Bring admit card and photo ID to Aptitude Test

APTITUDE TEST

Conducted simultaneously in Karachi and other cities

Test center, date, and reporting time indicated on Admit Card

Successful candidates qualify for Group Discussion & Interview

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

Intermediate / A' Level Certificate

Graduate Students:

Matriculation / O' Level Certificates

Intermediate / A' Level Certificates

Topics assigned for debate and discussion

Assesses communication, interpersonal skills, confidence, and leadership potential

INTERVIEW

Evaluates maturity, academic aptitude, motivation, interpersonal skills, and career focus

ENROLMENT

Submit original documents and attested copies:

Matric / O Levels certificate

HSC / A Levels certificate

Bachelor & Master degree transcripts

Work experience certificate (if applicable)

Migration certificate (if applicable)

Equivalence certificate (if applicable)
