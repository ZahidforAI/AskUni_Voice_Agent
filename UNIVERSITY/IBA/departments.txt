UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)

4-year program offering broad business education.

Students study business fundamentals along with social sciences and liberal arts in freshman and sophomore years.

Foreign language study is required over two semesters (Arabic, Chinese, French, German).

Includes Personal Effectiveness course for soft skills development.

Students specialize in Marketing, Finance, MIS, HRM, or Entrepreneurship.

Includes six-month on-the-job training for experiential learning and networking.

BS Business Analytics

4-year program focusing on analytics, data tools, and applied methodologies.

Emphasizes integration of data analytics with core business principles.

Core subjects include Business Intelligence, Data Visualization, Predictive Analytics.

BS Accounting & Finance (BSAF)

4-year program, introduced in 2013, specialized in accounting and finance.

Recognized by ICAP, ACCA, CIMA, and other professional bodies with available exam exemptions.

Aligns with CFA syllabus to facilitate exams.

Prepares students for corporate, finance, and accounting careers.

BS Computer Science (BSCS)

4-year rigorous CS education including programming, systems, and research.

Emphasizes machine learning, AI, quantum computation, computer vision, robotics, HCI, and computer architecture.

Graduates join industry, government, startups, or pursue higher studies globally.

BS Economics

4-year program providing solid theoretical and practical foundations in economics.

Final year includes economics research project applying quantitative tools to real-world problems.

BS Economics & Mathematics

4-year dual-focus program combining economics and mathematics.

Prepares students for positions in private/public sector, banks, development organizations, and research.

BS Mathematics

4-year program with flexibility to pursue concentrations in allied areas of science.

Focuses on theoretical, analytical, and computational skills for academia and industry.

Prepares students for advanced studies in mathematics and related fields.

BS Social Sciences & Liberal Arts

4-year program with majors in Psychology, Political Science, Media and Culture, or History.

Liberal Arts component covers Philosophy, Literature, Visual Arts, Mathematics, Religion, Environmental Sciences.

Develops skills in reading, writing, quantitative thinking, critical inquiry, and research.

Culminating Experience in final year involves original research project.

Includes foreign language study and two summer internships (social service and research).

GRADUATE PROGRAMS

MBA

Prepares young leaders for management positions in medium and large enterprises.

Executive MBA (EMBA)

Part-time, Level-7 qualification.

Immersive Block Model of learning for strategic leadership development.

Includes international immersion and industry exposure.

MS Computer Science (MSCS)

30 credit hours, with thesis or research survey option.

Offers four specialized tracks.

Curriculum aligns with IEEE/ACM guidelines.

Emphasizes practical and research skills in advanced computing fields.

MS Data Science (MSDS)

Focuses on machine learning, statistics, big data analytics.

Prepares students from CS and non-CS backgrounds for data-driven roles.

Includes foundational math, statistical, and computational courses, plus electives in deep learning, text analytics, computer vision, and social network analysis.

MS Development Studies

Interdisciplinary approach to development considering history, culture, politics, technology.

Provides methods, policies, and practical knowledge for sustainable societal development.

MS Economics

Emphasizes applied economics, theory, and quantitative methods.

Prepares students for careers in research, policy planning, universities, and multinational organizations.

MS Finance

Provides theoretical and applied finance knowledge in a global context.

Includes core and optional courses to prepare for research or finance industry roles.

Open to students from quantitative backgrounds (CS, Mathematics, etc.).

MS Islamic Banking & Finance

Addresses local and global Islamic finance industry needs.

Covers Shariah-compliant banking, jurisprudence, and economics.

Suitable for fresh graduates, practitioners, and Shariah scholars.

MS Journalism

1.5-year program at the Centre for Excellence in Journalism.

Digital, audio, and video journalism training.

Hands-on newsroom experience and international internship opportunities.

MS Management

Builds analytical skills for academic and applied research.

Specializations in Operations Management, Strategy, and Organization.

Focus on digital transformation, business consultancy, process mining.

Flexible full-time and part-time options.

MS Mathematics

Offers specialized training in pure and applied mathematics.

Eligibility: BE (any discipline) or BS/MSc (16 years) in Physics, Statistics, CS; minimum 60% marks.

MS Marketing

Develops skills in Marketing research, consumer insights, advertising, social media, and retail analytics.

Emphasizes qualitative and quantitative research and innovation.

MS General Psychology

Postgraduate psychology program with applications in health, business, education, HR, IT, and social care.

POSTGRADUATE / PHD PROGRAMS

PhD Computer Science

Offers specializations in AI, HCI, ICT, MIS, and others.

Focuses on original research, interdisciplinary work, and industry interaction.

PhD Economics

Solid foundation in theory, quantitative methods, and applied economics.

Prepares graduates for teaching, research, and roles in public/private sectors.

PhD Mathematics

Focuses on theoretical and applied mathematical research.

Eligibility: MS/MPhil in Mathematics or allied areas, GRE/IBA aptitude test, interview.
