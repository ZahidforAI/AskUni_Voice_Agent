INSTITUTE OVERVIEW

The Institute of Business Administration (IBA) Karachi offers undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate programs.
It provides students with world-class education, professional training, and research opportunities across multiple disciplines.

Note: Each department offers full-time, visiting, and adjunct faculty, supporting various undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate programs.

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) – 4-year program offering broad business education

BS Business Analytics – 4-year program focusing on analytics, data tools, and applied methodologies

BS (Accounting & Finance) – 4-year program introduced in 2013, specialized in accounting and finance

BS (Computer Science) – 4-year rigorous CS education, including programming, systems, and research

BS (Economics) – 4-year program providing solid theoretical and practical foundations in economics

BS (Economics & Mathematics) – 4-year dual-focus program combining economics and math

BS (Mathematics) – 4-year program emphasizing analytical and theoretical mathematics

BS (Social Sciences & Liberal Arts) – 4-year program with options to major in various social sciences or liberal arts subjects

GRADUATE PROGRAMS

MBA – Prepares young leaders for management positions in medium and large enterprises

Executive MBA – Part-time program for working professionals to acquire strategic leadership skills

MS (Computer Science) – 30 credit hours, thesis or research survey option

MS (Data Science) – Combines machine learning, statistics, and big data analytics

MS (Development Studies) – Interdisciplinary study exploring development theory and practices

MS (Economics) – Strong background in theory, quantitative methods, and applications

MS (Finance) – Theoretical and applied finance knowledge

MS (Islamic Banking and Finance) – Specialized program for Islamic finance industry

MS (Journalism) – Program at the Centre for Excellence in Journalism

MS (Management) – Prepares students for research and analytical roles in management

MS (Mathematics) – Advanced theoretical and quantitative methods

MS (Marketing) – Focus on marketing strategies, analytics, and industry relevance

MS (General Psychology) – Postgraduate program in psychology

POSTGRADUATE / PHD PROGRAMS

PhD Computer Science – Launched in 2005

PhD Economics – Launched in 2011

PhD Mathematics – Launched in 2011

These programs emphasize research, analytical skills, and professional development. They are designed to develop world-class scholars and leaders in their fields.

KEY NOTES

IBA Karachi offers diverse undergraduate and graduate programs with strong academic and professional support.

Students are guided by faculty across all 10 departments, supported by adjunct and visiting faculty.

Graduate programs include both tagged degrees (MBA) and untagged degrees (MS).

Postgraduate programs focus on PhD-level research and skill development.
