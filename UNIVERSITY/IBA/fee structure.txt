Transport for distances 0–20 km costs 45,000 PKR.

Transport for distances 21 km and onwards costs 65,000 PKR.

Hostel Fees (Per Semester):

Single occupancy room without AC costs 121,440 PKR.

Double occupancy room without AC costs 116,886 PKR.

Three or more occupancy rooms without AC cost 114,840 PKR.

AC single occupancy room costs 133,584 PKR.

AC double occupancy room costs 129,030 PKR.

AC room with three or more occupancies costs 125,235 PKR.

Fees for the Summer Semester for hostel residents who stay during summer will be charged on a prorate basis.

Utility charges for AC rooms will be applied separately on an actual and prorate basis.
