--- PAYMENT INSTRUCTIONS ---
1. Approved Banks: Fee must be paid ONLY at the Habib Metropolitan Bank (HMBL) or National Bank of Pakistan (NBP) branches located at the NED University Main Campus or TIEST.
2. Fee Voucher: Students must take a printout of the Fee Voucher from the portal and submit the fee ON or BEFORE the registration deadline (29th August, 2025).
3. Confirmation: After payment, submit the stamped (bank-stamped) department copy of the Fee Voucher to your department to receive an acknowledgement receipt.
4. Fee Status: Students are responsible for checking their fee status on the Student Portal after submission. If the status shows "Pending," contact the relevant bank the next business day.

--- ONLINE REGISTRATION PROCEDURE ---
Registration Portal: www.neduet.edu.pk/usms
Login Credentials: Use your student Portal account.

Key Registration Rules:
1. Course Selection: Students can ONLY select courses (regular or additional) for which they have passed the prerequisite course(s).
2. Registration Phases:
   - Phase-I: For Regular Course Registration.
   - Phase-II: For Backlog/Additional Course Registration.
   - NOTE: Both phases run in parallel for Fall 2025. A student with backlog courses can choose to skip them and generate a Phase-I Fee Voucher by giving consent on the Portal Dashboard.
3. Credit Hour Limit: Maximum allowed registration is 21 credit hours, inclusive of Additional/Backlog courses and Final Year Project.
4. B.Arch. Studio Limit: Architecture students cannot register for more than two design studio courses in any single semester.
5. Minimum Registration: In Phase-I, a student MUST register for at least ONE regular course and pay the full regular semester fee, plus any backlog course fees.
6. Course Timetable: Students may register for additional/backlog courses offered by any discipline, but they must consider the timetable to avoid clashes.
7. Equivalent Courses: Students may select an equivalent course if a required course is not being offered.
8. Locking Registration: Once the registration form is locked, students must contact their advisor to have it UNLOCKED. After making changes, they must log back in and lock the form again.

--- SPECIAL CATEGORIES & NOTES ---
Undergraduate Fellowship Programme:
- The policy and structure will remain the same as in the previous semester.

COD/ROD Applicants (Change/Re-admission of Department):
- Students applying for COD/ROD MUST register, pay fees, and attend classes in their CURRENT (parent) department until a final decision on their application is made.

Attendance Policy - VERY IMPORTANT:
- Class attendance will be marked and counted from the FIRST DAY of the semester.
- This applies to both Regular and Additional/Backlog courses.
- Attendance is NOT subject to the date a student completes their registration. Late registrants will miss attendance from day one.
