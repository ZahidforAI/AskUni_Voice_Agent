ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA:

1. DOMICILE REQUIREMENT:
Applicants must possess a Sindh domicile, except for candidates applying under specific nomination categories.

2. MINIMUM MARKS TO APPLY (PROVISIONAL):
Minimum 50% marks in HSC Part-I / O-Level or equivalent examination.
Minimum 57% marks required specifically for BE, B.Arch, and BS Computer Science programs.

3. PROVISIONAL ADMISSION WEIGHTAGE:
40% weightage: Marks obtained in HSC Part-I (or equivalent).
60% weightage: Score in the Pre-Admission Entry Test.

4. FINAL ELIGIBILITY FOR CONFIRMATION:
For BE, B.Arch, and BS Computer Science: Minimum 60% overall marks in HSC (Full).
For all other BS Programs: Minimum 50% overall marks in HSC (Full).

5. FOR O-LEVEL CANDIDATES:
Must submit the IBCC equivalence certificate at the time of application.

6. FOR A-LEVEL / 12TH GRADE CANDIDATES:
Must submit their final Grade Sheet by October 2025.
Must submit the IBCC equivalence certificate by mid-January 2026.

7. FOR DAE (DIPLOMA) CANDIDATES:
Must have completed their Diploma during the years 2022, 2023, or 2024.
Minimum 60% marks required in the Diploma.

8. FOR DIFFERENTLY-ABLED CANDIDATES:
Encouraged to apply if they meet the eligibility criteria.
