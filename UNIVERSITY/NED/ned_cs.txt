The Department of Computer Science and IT offers a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BS CS) program that prepares students for careers in the field of computer science and technology. The program aims to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and tools required to develop, design, and manage computer systems and software.

The BS CS program at NED University is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in computer science theory, as well as practical experience in programming, software development, data structures and algorithms, computer networks, database systems, and artificial intelligence. The program is structured to enable students to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills that are essential for success in the field of computer science.

Graduates of the BS CS program at NED University are highly sought after by employers in various industries, including software development, e-commerce, information technology, and finance. The program also provides an excellent foundation for pursuing advanced degrees in computer science or related fields.

Overall, the BS CS program at NED University is an excellent choice for students who want to pursue a career in computer science and technology, as it provides a rigorous and comprehensive education in the field, as well as practical experience and opportunities for research and innovation.
