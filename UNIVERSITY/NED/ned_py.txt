The Department of Physics is dedicated to promoting academic excellence, advancing high-quality research, and developing future leaders in the field of physics. Our academic programs provide students with a comprehensive foundation in fundamental physics while fostering critical thinking, analytical reasoning, and problem-solving skills essential for scientific inquiry and technological progress.

Graduates of the Department have established distinguished careers in academia, research institutions, and a broad spectrum of industries, including energy, healthcare, aerospace, and data science. These accomplishments are a testament to the commitment, expertise, and scholarly contributions of our faculty members, whose research encompasses diverse areas such as quantum physics, medical physics, environmental and climate studies, materials science, and computational physics.

To remain aligned with national priorities and international scientific advancements, the department continuously reviews and enhances its academic curricula and research activities. This ensures that our graduates are well prepared to address emerging scientific challenges and to contribute effectively to innovation and societal development. Whether pursuing careers in research, industry, or education, students benefit from a rigorous and supportive academic environment that promotes excellence and professional growth.

We look forward to welcoming you to the Department of Physics and to supporting your academic and professional aspirations.
