- Zakat & Financial Aid: Multiple scholarships specifically from Zakat Funds.
- Loan Schemes: NBP Loan Scheme.
- Need-Based Assistance: Disadvantaged Students Scholarship Programme (DSSP), HHRD Financial Assistance.
