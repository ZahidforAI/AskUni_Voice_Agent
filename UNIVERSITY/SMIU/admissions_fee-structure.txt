FEE STRUCTURE (Effective from Spring 2024)
Note: All amounts are in Pakistani Rupees (PKR).

--- FEE CATEGORIES AND DESCRIPTIONS ---
1.  Admission Fee: A one-time fee payable only at the time of admission.
2.  Security Deposit: A one-time refundable deposit payable at the time of admission.
3.  Registration / Enrollment Fee: A recurring fee payable in every semester.
4.  Lab / Library Fund: A recurring fee payable in every semester.
5.  Student Activity Fee: A recurring fee payable in every semester.
6.  Examination Fee: A per-course fee charged for each course enrolled.
7.  Tuition Fee: A per-credit-hour fee. Total tuition = (Credit Hours) x (Tuition Fee per Credit Hour).
8.  First Semester Fee / Fee at Admission: The total amount due at the start of the first semester. This includes Admission Fee, Security Deposit, and all semester-specific fees (Registration, Lab/Library, Student Activity, Examination for all courses, and Tuition for all credit hours).
