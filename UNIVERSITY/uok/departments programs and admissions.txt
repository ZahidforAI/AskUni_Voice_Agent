INSTITUTE OVERVIEW

The University of Karachi is a premier public research university in Pakistan, offering a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate programs across multiple faculties. It provides students with quality education, professional training, and research opportunities in diverse disciplines.

TEACHING & ASSESSMENT

Most departments follow the University's Semester System.

Assessment typically includes midterm/makeup tests, terminal examinations, lab work, assignments, presentations, and research projects.

Teaching methods include lectures, seminars, group discussions, field visits, and internships.

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES
Graduates from various departments find employment in:

Education & Academia: Schools, colleges, universities, research institutes.

Government & Public Sector: Civil services, planning commissions, geological surveys, environmental agencies, health departments.

Industry: Pharmaceutical, food, textile, chemical, petroleum, biotechnology, agriculture, IT & software.

Healthcare: Hospitals, clinical laboratories, rehabilitation centers.

Media & Communication: Journalism, advertising, public relations, digital marketing.

Legal & Justice System: Law firms, courts, criminology, corrections.

Private Sector: Banking, finance, business management, corporate security, consultancy.

NGOs & International Organizations: UNICEF, UNESCO, WWF, IUCN, UN agencies.

KEY NOTES

The University of Karachi is one of the largest and oldest universities in Pakistan.

It offers a comprehensive range of programs from undergraduate to doctoral levels.

The Faculty of Science is particularly extensive, with 21 departments and several renowned research institutes (e.g., ICCBS, KIBGE).

There is a strong emphasis on linking academic knowledge with industry needs and practical application.

Many departments offer both morning and evening programs to cater to different student needs.
