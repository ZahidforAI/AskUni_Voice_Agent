OVERVIEW OF FEE STRUCTURE

The University of Karachi charges tuition and admission fees on a per-semester basis for its Bachelor of Studies (BS) programs.
The fee amount varies depending on the faculty, department, program type, and in some cases morning or evening programs.
Some professional programs have significantly higher fees due to laboratory usage, professional accreditation, or clinical training.

FACULTY OF ARTS & SOCIAL SCIENCES

The Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences focuses on humanities, social sciences, communication, and behavioral studies.
These programs generally have lower to moderate fees compared to professional and medical programs.

The following BS programs charge 37,500 PKR per semester in both the 1st year and 3rd year:

FACULTY OF LAW, ENGINEERING & PHARMACY

This faculty contains professional and accredited programs, which generally have higher fees.

KEY INSIGHTS FOR AI EXPLANATION

Arts, languages, and religious studies are lowest-cost programs.

Business, law, pharmacy, AI, and medical-related programs are high-cost programs.

Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) has the highest tuition fee at UOK.

Evening programs generally cost more than morning programs.

Fees are charged per semester, not annually.

OVERVIEW OF PhD FEES AT UNIVERSITY OF KARACHI

The University of Karachi charges four main types of fees for PhD programs:

Processing Fee – paid at the time of application

Admission Fee – paid once upon provisional admission

PhD Semester Fee – paid every six months during enrollment

PhD Thesis Evaluation and Viva Examination Fee – paid at the final stage

These fees vary depending on the faculty, department, institute, and research facilities involved.

COMMON FEES APPLICABLE TO ALL LOCAL STUDENTS

The Processing Fee for all PhD programs is 8,000 PKR.

The Admission Fee (PhD) for all programs is 30,000 PKR.

The Thesis Evaluation and Viva Examination Fee for all programs is 85,000 PKR.

These three charges are uniform across all departments for local students.

FEES FOR FOREIGN PhD STUDENTS

Foreign students enrolled in PhD programs at the University of Karachi are charged higher semester fees due to international enrollment policies.

For foreign students in the following faculties:

Arts & Social Sciences

Management & Administrative Sciences

Islamic Studies

Education

Science

Engineering

Pharmacy & Pharmaceutical Sciences

The fee structure is:

Processing Fee: 8,000 PKR

Admission Fee: 30,000 PKR

PhD Semester Fee: 200,000 PKR per six months

The Thesis Evaluation and Viva Fee remains applicable as per university rules.

IMPORTANT NOTES FOR AI EXPLANATION

All semester fees are charged every six months, not annually.

Admission fee is one-time only.

Thesis and viva fee is paid at the final stage of the PhD.

Science, engineering, pharmacy, and medical-related programs are significantly more expensive than arts and humanities.

Physical Therapy has the highest PhD tuition cost.

Foreign students pay almost double the semester fee compared to local students.
